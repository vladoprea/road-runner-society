Cycloturistic Paths

Cycloturistic Pathways

Project`s scope is regional development and the revitalization of the Somes-Chioar area by creating a touristic infrastructure for practicing mountain cycle tourism.

Objectives

The objectives of this project are to promote Romania abroad as a destination for cycling experiences and encouraging the locals in practicing this form of eco tourism.

Action

We mapped over 100 km of rural and forest roads and succeed in interconnecting 72 km of possible cycloturistic pathways.

Creative Campus

Creative Campus

The first stage of this project is to reconstruct the ex-school from Durusa village which is inactive now and transforming it in an educational center in partnership with the Local Council, this way keeping the educational function of the building.

Objectives

The educational objective of the project will be reached since day 1 (through the construction process itself), its operating scope being widened with it completion, as being transformed in an educational center dedicated for conferences, workshops, trainings and other forms of education.
